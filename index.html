Usage

  • Begin by selecting a game and setting from the dropdown menus. Only the currently-available options are selectable. All planned games will be shown (and spoiled) here.
  • For upgrades and keys shown individually, click on them to toggle whether or not they've been collected.
  • For energy tanks, expansions, and keys shown numerically, click to increment the count, and Shift-click to decrement it.
  • To start or stop the timer, click the timer or press Enter.
Note that depending on the game, certain items won't count toward the item completion percentage.
